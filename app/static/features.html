Features

Our music practice platform is designed to help you improve your skills and make practice more enjoyable. Here are some of the features we're working on:

MP3 Upload & Sheet Music Generation

Upload your favorite MP3 files and our AI will automatically generate sheet music for you to practice with.

Beginner Mode

Our beginner mode is designed for new learners, with simplified exercises and interactive lessons to help you get started.

Personalized Practice Plans

Our AI will create a personalized practice plan based on your experience and progress, ensuring you're always challenged and improving.

Grading Mode

Record yourself playing and our AI will grade your performance, providing feedback on areas for improvement.

AI-powered Feedback

Get instant feedback on your playing, with our AI analyzing your performance and providing suggestions for improvement.

Community Sharing

Share your progress and connect with other musicians, getting feedback and inspiration from the community.
